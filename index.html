Controls & Shortcuts

Mouse Controls

  • Rotate View: Left-click + Drag
  • Pan View: Right-click + Drag
  • Zoom View: Scroll wheel
  • Move Atom: Ctrl + Left-click + Drag
  • Delete Atom: Ctrl + Right-click
  • Create/Increment Bond: Shift + Click atoms

Touchscreen Controls

  • Create/Increment Bond: Tap atoms
  • Delete Atom: Double-tap atom
  • Move Atom: Long-press + Drag

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Cancel Action: Esc
  • Cycle Helpers: ← / → (placement mode)
  • Confirm Placement: Enter (placement mode)
